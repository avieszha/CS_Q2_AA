PeriodiCastle is a whimsical and study-friendly website that transforms the periodic table into a Harry Potter-style design. Each element is “sorted” into one of the four Houses of Chemistry — Gryffindor (s-block), Slytherin (p-block), Hufflepuff (d-block), or Ravenclaw (f-block), in order to aid students and learners alike in remembering the structure of the blocks, the trends, and their respective properties. Users can hover on an element to reveal information about it or even use a sorting hat feature to classify an element by name, symbol, or property. After learning, they may also challenge themselves in Quiz Mode.

You may use the navigation bar found above, or refer to the Marauder’s Map in the footer, to guide you during your time here at PeriodiCastle. Explore more features like audio toggles or the Lumos spell as you walk around the wizarding world of Chemistry!
